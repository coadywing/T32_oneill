\section*{Facilities and Other Resources}

\subsection*{Office Space}

The three Program Directors and trainees are physically co-located on a single floor
within the O'Neill School, with doctoral student offices embedded within the same suite
as faculty offices. This arrangement facilitates daily informal interaction, rapid
feedback on research, and close mentoring beyond scheduled meetings.

\subsection*{Data Resources}

Trainees will have access to major datasets relevant to aging research, including:

\begin{itemize}
  \item Medicare claims data (through P01 and institutional data use agreements)
  \item Medical Expenditure Panel Survey (MEPS)
  \item Health and Retirement Study (HRS)
  \item Long-term care assessment data
  \item Linked survey-administrative datasets
\end{itemize}
